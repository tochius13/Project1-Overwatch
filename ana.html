Ana Snapshot

Ana is a Support hero who rewards aim and who counters alot of heros. Shes a healing sniper

Why I Love Ana

  • She can completely swing fights with her nade
  • She rewards good positioning and awareness.
  • Shes a good pick no matter who they pick.

My Play Style

  • I play from safe backline angles.
  • I try to hit as many nades as possible to prevent them from healing
  • You gotta save sleep dart for ultimates or an annoying tracer tryna flank you.
  • Nano is huge, the faster you get it, the better postion you'll be in later on.
