My favorite Tank. I love his carry potenital but his whole kit is behind his hook. His hook use to give people nightmares because of the way it use to be coded.

Roadhog

Roadhog is a Tank who can stay alive for a super long time, he is meant to be in your face, pick off squishies and just spam poke damage while healing him self.

Why I Love Roadhog

  • He can create pressure without needing constant healing.
  • He is super annoying to go against and super fun to be toxic with
  • You can really solo carry

My Play Style

  • I play corners and hold angles where hook threats matter.
  • I look for picks before full team fights start.
  • I save self-heal for when I know I can survive and keep space.
  • I try to peel with hook when my backline gets pressured.

My Highest Rank

Master (Tank)

Lore

Roadhog is a masked enforcer from Australia, known for surviving chaos and turning into a feared outlaw. He is often connected with Junkrat, and together they have a reputation for crime and destruction.

I like his vibe because he feels like a character who does not care about fear. He just walks forward and dares you to deal with him.

My Favorite Thing About His Kit

Chain Hook is my favorite part. It is simple, but it keeps the supports super scared of you at all times. One good hook can win a fight or force a team to play back.

  • Hook creates picks.
  • Hook forces respect and space.
  • Hook punishes bad positioning.
