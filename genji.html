Genji

Genji is a DPS who you need to be on your toes at all times, you need to be aware of ppl who are low health try to flank and get squishies and to stay alive.

Why I Love Genji

  • He has a super high skill ceiling, so he never gets boring.
  • He rewards smart engages, not just aim. Cause my aim lowkey trash
  • He can create big moments when you time your blade which is his ult and its super fun when your get a big blade

My Play Style

  • I poke first, jump around alot gotta stay moving then dive when I see someone low or out of position.
  • Gotta use walls and high ground to stay safe until it is time to commit.
  • I save Deflect for key threats, not random damage.
  • I try to finish fights fast, then reset and escape.
